About CHAIC

The Caribbean Health AI Congress (CHAIC) brings together leading healthcare professionals, AI researchers, and technology innovators for two days of transformative conversations, workshops, and networking in the heart of San Juan, Puerto Rico. Join us September 25–26, 2026 as we explore how artificial intelligence is reshaping medicine across the Caribbean and beyond.
